LTISC Inventory Management System - User Manual

The LTISC Inventory Management System is a comprehensive web-based application designed to streamline inventory tracking, warehouse management, purchasing operations, and employee attendance. The system uses Firebase for real-time data synchronization and provides role-based access control for different user types.

Inventory Management
  • Real-time stock tracking with daily updates
  • Weekly stock chart visualization
  • Stock level monitoring
  • Automated stock reports
Warehouse Management
  • Multi-warehouse support
  • Warehouse staff management
  • Location tracking
  • Inventory distribution
Purchasing Management
  • Purchase order creation and tracking
  • Vendor management
  • Budget monitoring
  • Procurement analytics
Attendance Management
  • Employee clock in/out tracking
  • Attendance reports
  • Schedule management
  • Performance analytics

Accessing the Dashboard: After logging in, you'll see the module selection page. Click on the module you want to access (Inventory, Warehouse, Purchasing, or Attendance). The dashboard displays key statistics and charts for your selected module.

Navigation Menu: Use the left sidebar to navigate between different sections within a module. The top bar displays your current role and provides quick access to settings and logout.

Tracking Stock: The system automatically captures daily stock snapshots at midnight. Stock levels are displayed in real-time and visualized in weekly charts. You can view historical trends and receive alerts when stock levels drop below thresholds.

Stock Charts: The weekly stock chart shows trends over the last 7 days. Missing data points appear as gaps in the chart, indicating days with no stock activity.

Managing Warehouses: Admins can create, edit, and delete warehouses. Each warehouse can have multiple staff members assigned and tracks inventory location.

Staff Assignment: Assign warehouse staff to specific locations and manage their access permissions. Track deliveries and material movements between warehouses.

Creating POs: Use the purchase order form to create new orders. Specify items, quantities, unit prices, and delivery dates. The system calculates total costs automatically.

PO Tracking: Monitor purchase order status (pending, confirmed, delivered, cancelled). View vendor performance and budget allocation across departments.

Recording Attendance: Employees clock in and out through the attendance module. The system automatically calculates hours worked and identifies late arrivals.

Attendance Reports: Generate detailed reports showing present/absent counts, late arrivals, and attendance patterns. Admins can manually adjust records and add notes.

User Management: Create, edit, and delete user accounts. Assign roles (admin, warehouse, purchasing, attendance) and manage user status (active/disabled).

Permissions & Restrictions: Set module-specific restrictions for individual users. Override default role permissions and create custom access policies.

System Settings: Configure system-wide settings, view activity logs, and manage security policies.

Cannot log in: Verify your email and password. Contact your admin if your account is disabled or doesn't exist.

No permission to access module: Your admin has restricted your access. Contact them to request module access.

Stock data not updating: The system captures stock at midnight. Ensure your browser time is synced and data has been entered in the inventory.

Data not syncing: Check your internet connection. Firebase requires stable connectivity for real-time updates.

Need Help? Contact your system administrator or check the activity logs for error messages. Most issues can be resolved by refreshing the page or clearing browser cache.
